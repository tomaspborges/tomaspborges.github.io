About me

PhD Student in Political Science at IESP-UERJ and Data Scientist at Quaest Pesquisa e Consultoria. In 2026, I am a visiting student at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT).

Over the past few years, I have worked across international organizations, policy research, and NGOs, focusing on the intersection between data science, public policy, and political behavior. I hold an MA in Political Science from IESP-UERJ and a BA in Applied Foreign Languages (International Business) from CEFET-RJ, including an exchange program at Institut d'études politiques de Strasbourg (Sciences Po Strasbourg).
